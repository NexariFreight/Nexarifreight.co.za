About Us

Nexari Freight is a Durban-based logistics company established in 2014, delivering dependable and efficient freight and cargo transportation solutions. Operating from the Jacobs Industrial Area, we have built a reputation for reliability, professionalism, and timely delivery across a wide range of logistics needs.

Our team is committed to ensuring that every shipment is handled with precision and care. Whether managing local distribution or larger cargo movements, we provide tailored logistics solutions designed to meet the evolving demands of modern businesses.

At Nexari Freight, we understand that logistics is the backbone of business operations. That’s why we prioritize efficiency, communication, and customer satisfaction in every delivery.
